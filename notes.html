Notes

This is information that was recorded in a set of old diaries. Not every year was present and the comments in the diaries were rather cryptic.

1972

Before getting married in 1972 I lived at 37 Wilmington Avenue, Alveston, Derby with John Starkey, and Colin Welsh. When I left my place was taken by John Taylor.

Put deposit on Plot 3, Barley Close Terrace, Little Eaton (being built by Messers T. Loach) on 6th March 1972.

24th July; moved into Flat 1, 115 Mill Hill Lane, Derby because the house will not be ready in time.

Wednesday 2nd August - Paul McNicholas and I have a joint stag night at the Dolphin in Derby.

Married at Chislehurst Methodist Church on Saturday 5th August 1972 at 2:30. Left Derby on Monday 7th for a week in the Lake District.

Monday 4th September; Angela's fist day at Nightingale Primary School in Derby.

Saturday 18th November 1972; moved into Plot 3, Barley Close Terrace.

1973 and 1974 diaries missing

1975

7th January; fist computer class as part of a City & Guilds Further Education Teachers course.

16th January; Uncle Sam's funeral.

Saturday 22nd February; Granddad Ludlam died.

Purchase of 61 Highfield Road, Lymm (for £13,200) delayed due to Liles purchase, so moved into 58 Lyndon close, Woolston on Friday 17th October. Started at NPC on the 20th. Worked for Alan Goode.

1976

5th January; Angela started at Bruche school. Finished there on 23rd July.

Monday 2nd February; moved into 61 Highfield Road, Lymm, WA13 0DT. Seem to have bought kitchen units and boiler before we moved. Left Lyndon Close on the Friday and installed boiler during that first week. JB helped me lift it on to the wall.

24th to 28th May; five days in the Lake District.

Friday 6th August to Friday 13th; a week in Wales.

Saturday 11th December, 2:55pm; Victoria born at 6lb 7½oz.

1977

5th January; replaced Mini Countryman with Austin Maxi.

Sunday 20th March; Victoria's Christening.

1978

Monday 6th February; started at Ferranti.

22nd May to 24th; Victoria taken into hospital with a high temperature.

10th October; during a long weekend in Wales we found and made an offer on Hillcrest, Mountain Road, LLanllechid.

1979

Drove to North Wales to check that the cottage was all right before exchanging contracts. However, there was snow and I had to leave the car on the main road and walk up to the village and then up to Mountain Road.

Friday 30th March; Awake at 2am and at the hospital by 4:30. Paul weighed in at 8lb 1oz at 9:25.

1980

Towards the end of May my first project with Ferranti was delivered to site (BP Barry in South Wales). The last week of June, following installation and hardware tests, we were on site for acceptance tests.

During September I prepared equipment for the Interkama 80 exhibition (liaising with Jürgen Frisch from the Wiesbaden office), going there in October. This was also the time that I got involved with Shell, initially on the Fulmar project.
